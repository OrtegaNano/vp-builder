## The Problem We Solve

Right now, most wealth management firms are stuck in one of three places — and all three are costing them.

### 1. Uncontrolled Experimentation

Employees are already using ChatGPT and other AI tools in ad hoc, potentially risky ways. There's no guidance on data privacy, no compliance framework, no quality control. It only takes one employee pasting sensitive client data into a public AI tool to create a regulatory incident. The firm doesn't have visibility into what's happening, and the longer it goes unchecked, the greater the risk.

### 2. Total Lockdown

Leadership has responded to the AI risk by restricting access entirely. No tools, no experimentation, no adoption. The firm is protected from risk — but it's also protected from every efficiency gain AI offers. Meanwhile, competitors are using AI to draft client communications in minutes, summarise research instantly, and run leaner operations. The gap widens every month.

### 3. Generic Training That Doesn't Stick

Some firms have tried to address the gap with off-the-shelf AI training. The problem: it's generic. It teaches employees what a large language model is and how to write a prompt, but it doesn't show them how to draft a suitability letter, summarise a fund factsheet, or streamline client onboarding. It doesn't speak their language, it doesn't cover their workflows, and employees forget everything within a week. The investment is wasted, and the firm is no closer to real AI adoption.

**AI Ready solves all three problems at once.** We give every employee the practical, hands-on skills to use AI tools in their actual day-to-day workflows — while teaching them exactly where the boundaries are. What's safe, what's not. What regulators care about, and how to stay on the right side of it.

## The "Too Good to Refuse" Offer (Hormozi Framework)

The value of any offer is determined by four variables:

**Value = (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood of Achievement) / (Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice)**

Here's how AI Ready maximises every lever:

### Dream Outcome (Maximise)

Every employee in the firm confidently using AI in their actual workflows. The firm operating measurably faster — hours recovered per employee per week. Zero compliance incidents from uncontrolled AI use. The C-suite can tell the board they've future-proofed the business. HR/L&D can show they delivered a programme with real adoption, not just attendance.

### Perceived Likelihood of Achievement (Maximise)

This is where most training courses fail. Buyers have seen employees sit through workshops and change nothing. Everything in our offer signals "this one actually works":

- **Wealth-management-specific examples** they'll recognise from their own desks — not generic scenarios they can't relate to.
- **Role-specific tracks** so every employee gets relevant content — removing the #1 reason corporate training fails (irrelevance).
- **The personalisation wizard** that tailors the experience from the first click — signalling premium quality and individual attention.
- **The AI readiness assessment** conducted before the course begins — showing we're tailoring to their firm's specific situation, not shipping a generic product.
- **Ready-made prompt libraries and templates** they can use immediately — proof that this delivers tangible tools, not just knowledge.

### Time Delay (Minimise)

The buyer should feel like their team gets value in the first week, not after a six-month rollout:

- The course is designed for practical application, not passive learning — skills transfer to real work within the first module.
- The personalisation wizard means no time wasted on setup or irrelevant content.
- The action plan at the end gives employees a clear 30-day path, not a vague "go figure it out."

### Effort & Sacrifice (Minimise)

The buyer's biggest fear at £997/employee is internal friction — people not completing it, having to chase adoption, or the course disrupting billable work:

- **Role-specific tracks** mean nobody wastes time on irrelevant material — the course is as efficient as possible.
